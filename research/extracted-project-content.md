## B. Project 01 — Designing with AI, Not by AI

**Meta:** slug `designing-with-ai` · Loại: AI Workflow / Thesis · Brand context: MWG (TGDĐ/ĐMX) · 7 ảnh
**Tagline:** *"Designing with AI, Not by AI"*
**Short:** A collection of real-world projects exploring how AI accelerates creative production while design judgment remains essential — across campaign design, character systems, and production assets.

**Mạch nội dung (theo ảnh, dùng làm case study tường thuật):**

1. **Hero** (img 1) — "Designing with AI / Not by AI" + 3 banner thật (FLYCO, game "Đập tan nắng gắt", smartwatch sale).
2. **One Tool. Different Levels of Execution.** (img 2) — 2 chế độ:
   - **Effort Optimization** — Prioritize speed & efficiency for short timelines & clear business objectives. Tín hiệu: *Short timeline · Clear objective · Low human involvement* (vd FLYCO, Xiaomi Buds 6).
   - **Creative Control** — Prioritize creative quality, consistency, impact through deeper human involvement. Tín hiệu: *Strong Visual Hierarchy · Campaign Importance · Human Refinement* (vd game Halloween, smartwatch sale).
3. **Effort Optimization — Streamlining Routine Tasks** (img 3, FLYCO):
   - Context: short-term banners generated efficiently with AI for standard requests.
   - Creative Direction: Clean & Premium · Summer Atmosphere · Minimal Distractions · Personal Care Category.
   - Why This Approach: Standard request · Clear objective · Production-Ready Output.
   - **Result: Output aligned with brand direction, deployed with zero manual rework.**
4. **Creative Control — Building a Character State System** (img 4):
   - AI accelerated exploration, but **game logic defined the final system**.
   - AI Exploration → Human Selection → Visual Consistency.
   - Reference character (sun mascot) → 4 selected states: **Angry** (extreme heat/start) · **Sweating** (cooling begins) · **Worried** (cooling down/near success) · **Relaxed** (cool & happy/success).
   - Quote: *"AI generated options. The character system came from the game concept and design decisions."*
5. **From Character States to Gameplay** (img 5): mỗi biểu cảm map vào 1 stage game (Stage 01 Extreme heat → 04 Cool & Relaxed). *"Character emotions were directly tied to gameplay progression."*
6. **Scaling Design Systems with AI — From Single Assets to Production-Ready Variations** (img 6):
   - Context: campaigns required dozens of thumbnail variations across brands, promotions, product categories (Sale theme · AI feature · Warranty badge · Tech theme).
   - Final deployment: selected, refined, aligned with brand requirements.
   - Benefits: More Options · Faster Iteration · Consistent System · Production Ready.
7. **Conclusion** (img 7): EXPLORE → CREATE → SCALE → **DECIDE (Human judgment ensures final quality)**. Tuyên ngôn: **"AI Generates Possibilities. Designers Make Decisions."**

**Result/Impact (rút ra):** zero manual rework, sản xuất hàng chục biến thể nhanh, hệ thống nhất quán — đóng khung AI như *hiệu suất + phán đoán thiết kế*.

## C. Project 02 — Halloween Campaign 2025 (Multi-Brand)

**Meta:** slug `halloween-multi-brand` · October 2025 · Seasonal Campaign · **Thegioididong × Dienmayxanh** · 8 ảnh
**Short:** A scalable seasonal campaign visual system shared across two brands while preserving each brand's identity.

- **Challenge:** Create a shared Halloween campaign system across Thegioididong & Dienmayxanh while preserving each brand's visual identity.
- **Scope:** 2 brands · Desktop + Mobile · Landing page ecosystem · Promotional assets · Interactive game.
- **My Responsibility:** Campaign visual system · Multi-brand adaptation · Interactive game design · Promotional asset production.
- **Outcome:** Unified campaign system · Distinct brand expressions · Shared interactive experience.

**Mạch nội dung:**
1. **Hero** (img 1) — TGDĐ purple "DEAL MA QUÁI sợ mà mêêê — giảm đến 50%".
2. **Overview** (img 2) — Challenge / Scope / My Responsibility / Outcome (như trên).
3. **One Campaign System. Two Brand Expressions.** (img 3): Shared Campaign Layer (Halloween Concept · Shared Game Experience · Shared Reward Mechanics) → **TGDĐ** (Purple Palette · Consumer Electronics) | **ĐMX** (Blue Palette · Home Appliances). *Design Reflection: Campaign identity was prioritized over strict brand-color consistency to strengthen Halloween recognition.*
4–5. **From One Hero Banner to a Complete Asset System** (img 4 TGDĐ, img 5 ĐMX): Hero Banners (Desktop & Mobile) · Promotional Section Headers (Flash Sale, Top Deal Sốc) · Category Taglines (**10+ variations**) · Web Placements (**10+ placements**) · Social Assets (**3+ editable templates**).
6. **One Game Framework. Multiple Reward States** (img 6): Core Game Flow: Entry Screen → Spin the Wheel → Reward Outcome. Reusable Reward Assets (reward titles, cards, out-of-stock state) + Campaign Placement Assets ("Vòng quay ma thuật Halloween").
7. **Campaign Identity vs Brand Identity** (img 7):
   - Current Approach — **Campaign Identity Focus**: strong Halloween atmosphere, unified purple-blue palette. (Strong seasonal recognition · Cohesive experience · Consistent mood)
   - Looking Back — **Brand Identity Opportunity**: would retain more of TGDĐ's yellow identity while keeping Halloween mood. (Stronger Use of Brand Yellow · Stronger Brand Typography · More Recognizable Brand Cues)
   - Quote: *"Every visual decision carries a trade-off. The goal is not to choose one over the other, but to find the balance that best serves both the campaign and the brand."*
8. **Two brand expressions** (img 8) — banner ngang TGDĐ (purple) + ĐMX (blue).

**Result/Impact:** 1 hệ thống → 2 brand · 10+ taglines · 10+ web placements · 3+ social templates · 1 game framework dùng chung desktop+mobile.

## D. Project 03 — iPhone 17 Pro Launch

**Meta:** slug `iphone-17-launch` · Sept 2025 · Product Launch Campaign · **TopZone × Thegioididong** · 9 ảnh
**Short:** A scalable, variable-driven campaign system delivering a unified iPhone 17 Pro launch across two brands, three phases, and two platforms — built to absorb last-minute Apple assets.

- **Challenge:** Adapt Apple's launch assets into a scalable campaign system across **two brands and three launch phases**.
- **My Responsibility:** Campaign adaptation · Visual system design · Multi-brand consistency · Landing page execution.
- **Constraints:** 2 brands · 3 launch phases · **Unknown final assets** · **18-hour turnaround**.

**Mạch nội dung:**
1. **Hero** (img 1) — iPhone 17 PRO key visual cam/đen, "Quào. Rất Quao!", CTA "Đăng ký nhận thông tin".
2. **Overview** (img 2) — Challenge / My Responsibility / Constraints + mockup landing 2 brand.
3. **Phase 1 — Extending the Launch Identity** (img 3): translate Apple's event visual language → launch-ready landing page. Apple's Event Assets / Visual Input: Color palette · Lighting treatment · Visual direction (TopZone countdown "Chờ đợi đến ngày chính thức công bố tại Mỹ").
4. **Phase 2–3: Adapting the System** (img 4): The Reveal (Interest Registration) → Pre-order (Conversion). Core visual system shared across phases; Adaptive content layer (messaging & offers evolve). *"Designed for consistency. Built for adaptation."*
5–6. **Campaign Rollout** (img 5 desktop, img 6 mobile): **2 Brands · 3 Launch Phases · 2 Platforms** (TopZone + Thegioididong).
7. **Designing the System** (img 7): scalable file structure + **variable-driven system**. Variable collection Dark/Light (TGDĐ #FFC800/#E2A600, TopZone #FF2222/#DB0000). **One Source of Truth** (Components · Variables · Design standards · Shared assets) → Production (Deployment, Technical handoff) | Review (Presentation-ready, Stakeholder review). *1 Source of truth · 2 Output flows · 1 Goal: Fast, consistent execution.*
8. **Built for the Reveal** (img 8): Official assets available only after Apple's keynote.
   - Challenge: assets released after keynote · multiple rollout scenarios · limited deployment window.
   - System Response: variable-driven system · rapid asset replacement · multi-brand consistency.
   - Outcome: **2 Brands** (TopZone & Thegioididong) · **3 Launch Phases** (Teasing & Countdown / The Reveal — Instant Deployment / Pre-Order Go-Live) · **Live Before Competitors — landing pages launched at 06:00 AM**.
9. **Final summary** (img 9) — "iPhone 17 Pro Launch — a scalable campaign system delivering a unified brand experience across TopZone and Thegioididong."

**Result/Impact:** Live trước đối thủ lúc 06:00 AM · xử lý asset chưa biết trước trong 18h · 1 system → 2 brand × 3 phase × 2 platform, không hi sinh tính nhất quán.
